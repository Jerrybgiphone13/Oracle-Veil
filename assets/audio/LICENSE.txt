Files are grouped by category, matching the app's sound "kind" names.
Each category holds several near-identical takes of the same physical
action; the app picks one at random per play so repeated sounds don't
feel identical. Numbers below map to <category>/<n>.ogg.

  shuffle/ <- card-slide-1.ogg .. card-slide-8.ogg
             (one shuffle sound plays per card that starts moving
             during a shuffle gesture, so the amount of sound scales
             with the amount of cards actually swept)
  cut/     <- card-place-1.ogg .. card-place-4.ogg
  gather/  <- card-shove-1.ogg .. card-shove-4.ogg
  spread/  <- card-fan-1.ogg, card-fan-2.ogg
  take/    <- cards-pack-take-out-1.ogg, cards-pack-take-out-2.ogg
  flip/    <- cards-pack-open-1.ogg, cards-pack-open-2.ogg
